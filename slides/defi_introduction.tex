\begin{frame}{Definition}
\vspace{0.5em}
DeFi employs  public \textbf{Blockchain networks} and \textbf{smart contracts} to build \textbf{open}, \textbf{transparent}, \textbf{composable} and \textbf{non-custodial} financial protocols. 

	\begin{table}
	\scriptsize 
	\centering
		\begin{tabular}{rll}
			\uncover<2->{\pbox{0.7cm}{\includegraphics[width=0.07\textwidth]{../assets/images/globe.png}} &	{\normalsize Open} &  \pbox{7cm}{By default – no access restrictions.\\ Not only on Blockchain but also on protocol level, i.e., no KYC, no whitelisting, no minimum amounts.}\\[1cm]}
			\uncover<3-> {\pbox{0.8cm} {\centering \includegraphics[width=0.07\textwidth]{../assets/images/find.png}} & {\normalsize Transparent} & \pbox{7cm}{Mathematically verifiable by anyone. \\ All relevant logic is on-chain with every step \\ of a process transparently defined in code.} \\ [1cm]}
			\uncover<4->{\pbox{0.7cm}{\includegraphics[width=0.07\textwidth]{../assets/images/construction.png}} & {\normalsize Composable} & \pbox{7cm}{Interoperable lego pieces. Protocols do not discriminate between human or other smart contracts as users. Thus, they can be combined to new services or made accessible via different applications.} \\ [1cm]}
			\uncover<5->{\pbox{0.68cm}{\includegraphics[width=0.07\textwidth]{../assets/images/non-custodial.png}} & {\normalsize Non-Custodial} & \pbox{7cm}{No trust requirements. Due to the full-transparency of the smart contract logic in combination with a public blockchain as settlement layer.} \\[1cm]}

		\end{tabular}
		\label{tbl:simpletable}
	\end{table}

\end{frame}
